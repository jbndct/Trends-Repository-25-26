Project Identity

Centralized Web Platform 2026

iSkonek is a web-based scholarship listing and matching platform designed specifically for Filipino students. Developed by John Benedict Baladia, Arwen Fajardo, Alezandra Hertz Fresnido, and Kirsten Gail Querubin.

Problem Statement

The Scholarship Gap, 2026

Filipino students struggle with fragmented information. iSkonek centralizes listings and uses automated matching to connect students with opportunities they qualify for.
